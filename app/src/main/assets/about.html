About

Ad Wheel gives you the control over the ads. You can play and tweak with all the publicly available settings AdMob uses to render advertisements.

Features:

  • View mainstream application advertisements by random topic by spinning a topic wheel.
  • Control and view a particular ad by keywords using the search button.
  • Set your personal settings to see how they affect which ads get delivered.
  • View your ad viewing history by topics and time.

Disclaimer

Note that Ad Wheel *attempts* to find the most appropriate ad matching the user's search query by directly providing the provided-keywords into Google's Ad Engine. There is no guarantee that an ad will come back matching, or remotely matching, the user's original query (though it will attempt to.
